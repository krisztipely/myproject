A Bunny's Poem

In fields of green where carrots grow,
A bunny hops with joy, to and fro.
With cotton tails and floppy ears,
They nibble on lettuce without any fears.

They wiggle their noses, oh, so cute,
Their furry paws make no dispute.
In Bunny Paradise, they play and roam,
Creating smiles and a happy home.
